Bad for Business

Alright there, yer one of them big fancy heros aint ya.

As' Grogg got an adveture for you.

Head to the forrest of voices, south of here, rumors have it that theres something or someones attacking travellers.

Bad for business it is.

Deal with whatever it is and there will always be a bed and beer for you are the Drunken Pony...

- Grogg, owner of the Drunken Pony Inn

Instructions

  • Click the "Travel to the Forest" button you will be taken the encounter page.
  • You can also see you player stats to the right of the scroll

Encounter Page

  1. Click the "Fight em!" button to start the encounter.
  2. You'll be given instructions on what dice to roll on the scroll.
  3. click the button in the dice table to roll the dice.
  4. Player Stats will be added/removed from your dice rolls.
  5. The enemy will roll at the same time, whoever rolls the highest wins.
  6. Who ever reaches 0 health first loses the encounter.
